About Us

We want students to succeed!

Our Mission

Here at Student Tutor, we understand how hard it can be for students to receive help outside of the classroom. Most of the time, if one person has a question, another person is asking the same question but are too afraid to ask. We strive to create an environment where students can receive help 24/7. The best part is, students will be the ones answering your questions! We believe that this will create an environment of learning and collaboration rather than a website that simply has test answers on it.

62% of students are too afraid to ask questions in class based on the fear of being judged.

Our Story

All of us at Student Tutor are currently enrolled at Brigham Young University. We personally understand how hard it is for students to 1. Get all of their questions answered in the classroom, and 2. Find answers when you aren't in the classroom. We want to be able to help students that struggle with the same problems we have. We want to make it easier for us to make study groups, connect with students in our classes, and make learning a collaborative experience.
